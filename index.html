Capture your symptoms and let Relevo remember them for you

  • Snap, speak, or type

    Photograph prescriptions, dictate what happened at your appointment, or type it in. Relevo extracts and organises the clinical detail so you don't have to.

  • Post-appointment capture

    After every consultation, a short guided prompt helps you record what was discussed, what changed, and what to do next.

  • A picture that builds over time

    Every input strengthens your health record so doctors gets the information they need according to their area of expertise.

Understand your health and spot what's missing

  • Plain-language health view

    Relevo explains your conditions, medications, and history clearly, avoiding clinical jargon.

  • Gap detection

    Relevo monitors for unfollowed referrals, tests without results, and medication changes that don't add up, and flags them before your next appointment.

Walk into every appointment prepared; walk out with everything you need

  • Pre-appointment summary

    Tell Relevo why you're going and who you're seeing. It highlights the relevant history, flags gaps, and suggests what to raise.

  • One-page clinical summary via QR code

    Your doctor scans a code and sees a clear, filtered summary, with relevant background, current medications, and flagged concerns.
